NaijaHacks Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Do I Need Experience To Attend?

No experience necessary. We will have plenty of mentors and resources, along with several workshops targeted for beginners.

Does It Cost Anything?

Our sponsors have made sure NaijaHacks is 100%. You don't have to spend a dime!

What Should I Bring?

You should bring your jotting materials, and/or your laptop. A light lunch will be provided also.
Hackers

Are There Team Applications This Year?

Yes, hackers with similar solution to a problem can form a team for better efficiency.

What Kind Of Workshops, Talks, And Activities Will Be There?

We will have workshops and talks on Blockchain, Artificial Intelligence and General Software Development.

What Kind Of Hardware Will Be Provided At The Event?

Hackers will provide all hardware they need to build their software. However, documents and any necessary documentation will be shared to all hackers if needed.
